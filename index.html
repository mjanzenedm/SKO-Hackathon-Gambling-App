How the matcher scores

Risk fit

Matches games and venues to selected risk tolerance. Low-risk profiles are steered toward lower minimums and longer-session games.

Income fit

Uses income as a bankroll sensitivity proxy, favoring lower-minimum properties for lower bands and allowing more premium venues for higher bands.

Skill fit

Rewards skill-heavy options such as live poker only when the selected skill level can plausibly support them.

Time vs payout

The slider shifts weighting between longer-session characteristics and payout-upside characteristics without presenting guaranteed returns.
